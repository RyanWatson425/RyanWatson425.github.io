My Experience

In the summer of 2023 I began creating an Android mobile application as part of an Electrical Engineering research project with a professor at SPU. The purpose of this application was to transfer data and signals with a custom piece of hardware that my professor had created. This involved doing embedded systems programming on the hardware and visualizing health data in a user friendly way on the application. This research continued into the fall and culminated in writing and submitting an abstract to multiple conferences. This research is still in the review process.
